The perodic tableis a tabular arrangementof chemical elements.Each element is represented by its chemical symbol such as Fe for iron,H for hydrogen ,and O for oxygen.Elements are classified into groups and periods based on their properties.

Chemical Equations

Chemical reactions are represented using chemical reactions.Here's an example of a chemical equations:

2H2 + O 2 → 2H2O

This equations represents the reaction between hydrogen gas (H2) and oxygen gas (O2) to form water (H2O)

Important Concepts

Stoichiometry is the calculations of reactants and products in chemical reactions. It involve balancing chemical equations to ensure mass and charges are conserved.

Subscriptsindicate the number of atoms of each element in a molecule,while superscriptsrepresents charges or the number of atoms in an ion.
